Engineering-Led Execution Model

Led by a qualified Electrical Engineer, we combine academic design rigor with field-proven technical teams to deliver complex industrial projects.

1. Engineering Design

Partnerships with university engineering faculties for planning, load studies, and detailed design work.

2. Technical Execution

Qualified technicians and artisans handle installation, wiring, and commissioning under engineering supervision.

3. Project Management

Single point of accountability. Safety files, QA/QC, and compliance managed to RBIDZ standards.

4. Local Delivery

Richards Bay-based crews. Rapid mobilization for shutdowns and emergency works within the IDZ.
